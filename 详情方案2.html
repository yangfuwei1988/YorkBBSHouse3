项目亮点

Guests will be immediately impressed by Studio 2’s artistic outside construction, which makes it resemble stacked boxes with vibrant colour accents. There is a sleek lobby, which boasts modern artwork and unique furniture pieces.


There are many places to catch up with old friends and meet new friends in Studio 2. Residents can entertain guests in the lavish dining room, which boasts an attached kitchen servery, or enjoy a classy cocktail in the sophisticated and nightclub-reminiscent Bar at Studio 2. For those who prefer a quieter night in, the media lounge is full of comfortable seating and features a wall of windows for a romantic view of the city at night. The billiards room, elegantly furnished and garnished with a chandelier, is a great place to enjoy a friendly match with friends or get acquainted with new neighbours. On warm nights, residents can head out onto the terrace.


For those who like to stay fit and active, there is a fully-equipped exercise room and a yoga and Pilates studio with change rooms.
